# LUXE Store Policies

## 1. Shipping & Delivery
* **Standard Shipping**: Free on all orders over $500. Delivery takes 3-5 business days.
* **Express Shipping**: Available for $25. Delivery takes 1-2 business days.
* **International Shipping**: We ship globally. Flat rate of $50 for international orders. Customs and duties are the responsibility of the customer.
* **Order Tracking**: Once an order is shipped, a tracking number is provided via email and visible in the VIP Dashboard.

## 2. Returns & Exchanges
* **Return Window**: Items can be returned within 30 days of the delivery date.
* **Condition**: Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have all original tags attached.
* **Process**: To initiate a return, contact support or use the VIP Dashboard. A prepaid return label will be provided.
* **Refunds**: Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after the returned item is received and inspected.
* **Exchanges**: We offer free exchanges for different sizes or colors of the same item.

## 3. Sizing & Fit
* **Women's Clothing**: True to size. European sizing standard.
* **Men's Clothing**: Tailored fit. If you prefer a looser fit, we recommend sizing up.
* **Accessories**: Bags are one-size. Belts should be ordered one size larger than your pant size.

## 4. Product Care
* **Cashmere**: Dry clean only or hand wash cold with mild detergent. Lay flat to dry.
* **Silk**: Dry clean recommended. Can be hand washed in cold water. Do not wring.
* **Leather**: Keep away from direct sunlight and heat. Use a leather conditioner every 6 months. Clean with a damp cloth.
